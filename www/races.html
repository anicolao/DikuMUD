Races of the Dying World

The races of Barsoom reflect the diverse inhabitants of a dying world. From the dominant Red Martians to the alien Kaldanes, each race brings unique strengths and cultural heritage. Choose wisely—your race will shape your abilities, your social standing, and your role in the grand tapestry of Martian civilization.

Red Martians

The Civilized Race

Copper-red skinned with coal-black hair, Red Martians are the most populous and civilized race. They organize into great city-states like Helium, Zodanga, and Ptarth, valuing honor, courage, and learning. Long-lived and culturally sophisticated, they dominate Martian civilization.

Stat Modifiers: None (baseline race)

Size: Medium (6 feet tall)

Best Classes: Any—most versatile race

Special Abilities:

  • None (balanced race)
  • Can excel at any class equally

Perfect for new players seeking flexibility

Green Martians

Four-Armed Warriors

Towering fifteen feet tall with olive-green leathery skin and four powerful arms, Green Martians are the nomadic warriors of the dead sea bottoms. With tusks and eyes set far apart, they follow a harsh culture forbidding emotional displays where strength determines all.

Stat Modifiers: +2 STR, +1 CON, -2 CHA, -1 INT

Size: Large (15 feet tall)

Best Classes: Warrior (excellent), Noble (acceptable)

Special Abilities:

  • Four-armed combat (extra attacks)
  • Environmental resistance (deserts, low water)

Inspired by Tars Tarkas and the Tharks

White Martians (Therns)

The False Gods

Tall and fair-skinned with golden hair and blue eyes, the Therns once posed as the gods of Barsoom. Though their religious fraud was exposed, they retain formidable combat skills and deep knowledge of ancient secrets. Proud and manipulative, they struggle to adapt to a world that has rejected them.

Stat Modifiers: +1 WIS, +1 CHA, -1 STR, -1 CON

Size: Medium (6-7 feet tall)

Best Classes: Noble, Scientist

Special Abilities:

  • Deception expertise
  • Religious/cultural knowledge
  • Natural authority

Masters of manipulation and ancient lore

Black Martians (First Born)

Pirates of the Sea of Omean

Coal-black skin and superior physiques mark the First Born, who rule the underground Sea of Omean. Elite warriors and aristocrats, they consider themselves the master race of Barsoom. Arrogant but skilled, they excel in naval combat and martial prowess.

Stat Modifiers: +1 STR, +1 DEX, +1 CHA, -2 WIS

Size: Medium (6-7 feet tall)

Best Classes: Warrior, Noble, Assassin

Special Abilities:

  • Superior combat training
  • Naval expertise
  • Natural leadership presence

The self-proclaimed master race

Yellow Martians

Keepers of the Pole

Yellow-skinned and culturally isolated, the Yellow Martians inhabit the frozen North Pole in hidden cities. Technologically advanced and xenophobic, they guard their secrets jealously. Their extreme isolation has made them both brilliant inventors and deeply suspicious of outsiders.

Stat Modifiers: +2 INT, +1 DEX, -1 CHA, -1 STR, -1 CON

Size: Medium (5-6 feet tall)

Best Classes: Scientist, Assassin

Special Abilities:

  • Cold resistance
  • Advanced technology knowledge
  • Stealth in polar environments

Brilliant but isolated inventors

Kaldanes

Disembodied Intellects

Perhaps the strangest race on Barsoom, Kaldanes are heads the size of human bodies, moving on spider-like legs. They can detach and ride humanoid bodies (Rykors), controlling them like vehicles. Pure intellect without emotion, they are master scientists but alien to normal social interaction.

Stat Modifiers: +3 INT, +2 WIS, -3 STR, -3 CON, -3 CHA

Size: Small (head-sized)

Best Classes: Scientist (only practical choice)

Special Abilities:

  • Body-riding (control Rykors)
  • Superior intellect
  • Telepathic communication
  • Immune to emotion-based effects

Pure intellect in bizarre form

Lotharians

Masters of Mental Projection

Pale and ethereal, Lotharians are masters of creating mental illusions so powerful they can temporarily manifest as physical reality. Living in isolation, they have developed incredible psychic powers at the cost of physical vitality. Few in number, they are sophisticated but fragile.

Stat Modifiers: +2 INT, +2 WIS, +1 CHA, -2 STR, -3 CON

Size: Medium (5-6 feet tall, slender)

Best Classes: Scientist, Noble

Special Abilities:

  • Create illusions
  • Mental projection
  • Psychic abilities
  • See through deceptions

Powerful minds in fragile bodies

Earthmen

Visitors from Jasoom

Humans from Earth (called Jasoom on Barsoom) who have somehow transported to Mars. In the lower gravity, they possess incredible strength and agility. Cultural outsiders who must prove themselves, they often bring fresh perspectives to ancient Martian problems.

Stat Modifiers: +2 STR, +1 CON, +1 DEX, -1 CHA (outsider)

Size: Medium (5-7 feet tall)

Best Classes: Warrior (excellent), Any (good)

Special Abilities:

  • Gravity advantage (enhanced strength/jumping)
  • Rapid adaptation
  • Outsider perspective (bonus to certain skills)

Inspired by John Carter himself

Race Selection Guide

For New Players

Red Martians are the perfect starting race—balanced, versatile, and able to excel in any class without penalties.

For Warriors

Green Martians and Earthmen make exceptional warriors, with massive strength bonuses. Black Martians combine strength with social grace.

For Scientists

Kaldanes are the ultimate scientists despite their bizarre form. Yellow Martians and Lotharians also excel with high intelligence.

For Nobles

White Martians and Lotharians have natural charisma and wisdom. Black Martians combine nobility with martial prowess.

For Assassins

Yellow Martians excel at stealth and precision. Black Martians combine deadly skills with aristocratic bearing.
