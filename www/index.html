A World of Adventure

Based on the fantastic Barsoom series by Edgar Rice Burroughs, this world is a rich tapestry of warring city-states, nomadic tribes, and horrifying monsters. From the towering green warriors of the dead sea bottoms to the decadent, technologically advanced red Martians, adventure and danger lurk behind every corner. Will you ally with the noble houses of Helium, or seek your fortune in the treacherous back-alleys of Zodanga? The choice is yours.
