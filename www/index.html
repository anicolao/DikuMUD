The Story of Barsoom

Mars is a dying world. Its once-great oceans have evaporated, leaving behind vast dead sea bottoms covered in ochre moss. Ancient civilizations struggle for survival, maintaining the thin atmosphere through colossal factories scattered across the planet.

The dominant Red Martians live in great city-states like Helium, Zodanga, and Ptarth, bound by codes of honor and martial prowess. The savage Green Martians, fifteen feet tall with four arms, roam the ancient ruins in nomadic hordes. In the frozen polar regions, the Yellow Martians guard their technological secrets, while the Black Pirates of the Sea of Omean rule the underground waterways.

This is a world where swordplay and scientific achievement go hand in hand, where airships cross the dying planet, and where honor matters more than life itself.

What Makes Barsoom Unique

Unlike traditional fantasy MUDs, Barsoom offers:

  • Science Fiction Setting: Advanced technology replaces magic—radium weapons, invisibility cloaks, matter transmitters, and brain transplant surgery
  • Honor-Based Culture: Your reputation and adherence to the Martian code of honor shape your journey
  • Diverse Civilizations: Each city-state and race has unique customs, technologies, and political intrigue
  • Epic Scale: From the canals of ancient Mars to the underground Sea of Omean, adventure spans an entire dying world
  • Rich Lore: Based on 11 novels by Edgar Rice Burroughs, with deep backstory and consistent world-building
